My Keynote Topics

From Depression to Transformation

Hear my personal story of tragedy to triumph, from a Year 10 high school dropout, domestic violence, drug and alcohol abuse, years of depression and anxiety to qualified accountant and successful entrepreneur and business owner.

Motivational, inspiring and thought provoking for any team or organisation wanting to show their audience that anything is possible when we chose to put our mind to it and change the way we think.

Content and Value

  • Why your attitude and energy matters
  • How to be self aware and understand emotional intelligence and why it is important to you
  • Understand goal setting
  • How you are wired to grow and give
  • Know how to live in a state of gratitude

Cash flow is King

Why is cash flow so important to our business?

Are you flying blind financially?

Do you know your business numbers?

Discover how to create a strategy to manage debtors, budgets and your cashflow.

Differentiate between profit and cashflow and create CASH FLOW CONFIDENCE!

Content and Value

  • Get clarity on cash flow and how it works
  • How cash flow solves long-term business trading
  • Why creating cash flow confidence

5 Steps to Effective Time and Staff Management

Learn how to value your time as a leader, create systems and procedures to effectively delegate to staff.

Free up your time and find out how to create a healthy working environment free of overwhelm and stress.

Content and Value

  • How to schedule and quickly execute staff delegation tips and tricks
  • Understanding how your communicate effects everything you do
  • The role and value of great leadership
  • How to spot time wasters and avoid overwhelming, elaborate procedures
  • How to lead not control
  • Simple healthy working environmental steps you can take home now to increase productivity
