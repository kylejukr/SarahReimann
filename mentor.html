Adelaide Business Mastermind

Adelaide Business Mastermind is a joint initiative between Michael Faraonio and Sarah Reimann. Founded in 2018, the groups have been highly successful in delivering small business owners valuable advice on how to grow, develop and build a successful business.

The groups provide an opportunity to come together once a month to discuss, to deliberate and to learn and develop, in a confidential environment. As much as it is about each and every person being able to consider their own development, it is a chance for each company leader to reflect, to build knowledge and capability and at the same time, support their peers. It is a supportive network of peers which are fundamental to both personal and business growth.

Benefits:

  • One, 2 hour meeting every month.
  • Each group has a maximum of 6 participants.
  • One member each month presents to the group about their issues, problems, struggles in their business.
  • A facilitator to assist discussion and solutions
  • Opportunity to give feedback and advice to the business owner that month and discuss solutions with your peers.
  • Business of the month has a take away action plan list
  • Meetings held in different locations around Adelaide in cafes, offices and organisations (see location list and times below)
  • Meetings are confidential to members
  • Accountability each month to the business owner who presented in the month prior
  • 30-minute pre group discussion with your facilitator to prepare for your presentation that month
  • 30-minute post group discussion with facilitator to give feedback and advice
